# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 3, 2025**

## Introduction

RDCPartner ("we", "our", or "us") operates the GOTime extension for Azure DevOps. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our extension.

## Information We Collect

### Data Collected by the Extension

- **Work Item Data**: We access work item information (ID, title, state, project) that you interact with through the extension
- **Time Tracking Data**: Time entries you create including dates, hours, notes, and associated work items
- **User Information**: Your Azure DevOps user ID and display name for time entry attribution
- **Project Information**: Project IDs and names where the extension is used
- **Organization Information**: Organization/host information for license validation purposes

### Data Storage Location

All data collected by the GOTime extension is stored:
- **Within Azure DevOps**: Time entries and settings are stored in your Azure DevOps organization's extension data storage
- **License Server**: Minimal license validation data (organization name, host, installation date, user counts) is sent to our license server for license management purposes

## How We Use Your Information

- **Time Tracking**: To enable time entry and reporting functionality
- **License Management**: To validate and manage extension licenses
- **Support**: To provide technical support and respond to your inquiries
- **Improvement**: To improve the extension's functionality and user experience

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

- **License Server**: Organization and installation data for license validation (organization name, host, user count, project count)
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect our rights
- **Business Transfers**: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data stored within Azure DevOps extension data
- Delete your time entries through the extension's data management features
- Contact us for data-related inquiries at support@rdcpartner.com

## Data Retention

- **Time Entries**: Stored in your Azure DevOps organization until you delete them
- **License Data**: Retained for license management and compliance purposes
